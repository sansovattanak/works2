#LOVEISDIVERSITY

Campaign Design


With the support from UN Women, #loveisdiversity is a online platform focussed on promoting the rights of the Cambodian lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, intersex and queer (LGBTIQ).

The Result – The platform, dubbed LOVEISDIVERSITY, aims to engage youths, especially students, in increasing society’s acceptance of LGBTIQ people and the profile of LGBTIQ issues. It was launched on 8 June in a ceremony at Pannasastra University of Cambodia in Phnom Penh. The ceremony was a session of Japanese Professor Kasumi Nakagawa’s Gender Studies class; about 180 people attended, mostly students and including some Buddhist monks.
